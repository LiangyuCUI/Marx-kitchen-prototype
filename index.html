The Art of cooking

Molecular gastronomy is a branch of food science that utilizes the principles of chemistry,physics and biology to develop delicious food that an be presented in new and interesting ways—solid cocktails, fruit jelly caviar, or vegetable foams and bubbles.It’s basically the science of food you thought could only exist in Willy Wonka’s chocolate factory.
